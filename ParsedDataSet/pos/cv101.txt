Assassin Game + Pirate Theme + Naval Combat + Stunning Visuals = Black Flag !This game is simply epic. The visuals are stunning and very much improved over it's predecessor (AC III).Naval Combat - The best part of this game is naval combat .You can upgrade your own ship & can ride with it wherever you want , you can attack other ships for loot and can take their crew.Story - Story is above average , Not the best but certainly not the worst.You will have no problem with it.Gameplay - The Gameplay is awesome , Naval combat is Excellent , Open world which is designed beautifully by Ubisoft. There are plenty of things to do other than story missions. Combat is great & Variety Of Missions.I loved the Soundtrack also .The presentations is awesome & you will love pretty much everything about this game , fully enjoyable.
