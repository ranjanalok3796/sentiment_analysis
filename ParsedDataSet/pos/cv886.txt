Hacking is your weapon, Ubisoft has built a great game and lives up to the hype created around it. Too sad few gamers are comparing it with GTA V and Assassin's creed when its clearly not. Besides a great campaign there is a lot to do in the Open World. But the best part is Online Multiplayer. 1v1 Online Hacking, Tailing happens so seamlessly that you don't even know when a player entered your game.
