Welcome to Chicago. Where country's most advanced computer system controls almost every piece of city technology and holds key information on all of the city's residents. You play as Aiden Pearce, a brilliant hacker but also a former thug, whose criminal past lead to a violent family tragedy. Now on the hunt for those who hurt your family, you'll be able to monitor and hack all who surround you.This is awesome open world hacking game. The main story is awesome. Side missions are also good. As the back cover says "Hacking is your weapon". You can hack almost everything in the city. You can stop traffic lights, download personal information, turn off the electrical grid and more. Graphics are good. Tech lovers and stealth killers will love this game for sure. Don't compare this game with Grand Theft Auto V (PS4). There is Version 1.05 (117 MB) patch that fixes some bugs. According to HLTB, the main story is around 18.5 hours.Thanks for reading.
